A . P . J Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

Missile Man Of India

A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an iconic Indian scientist, engineer and statesman who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. Often referred to as the "Missile Man of India", Kalam played a pivotal role in the country's space program and nuclear weapons tests. As President, he was known as the "People's President" for his accessible and inspiring leadership style. Kalam was the recipient of India's highest civilian honor, the Bharat Ratna, and authored several bestselling books, including his acclaimed autobiography "Wings of Fire". Throughout his life, Kalam remained deeply committed to education and empowering the youth of India to pursue careers in science and technology. His life and legacy continue to be celebrated across the country for his immense contributions to India's development as a modern, scientific powerhouse.
